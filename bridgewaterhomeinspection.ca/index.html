About Us

Bridgewater Home Inspections is a fully insured Winnipeg based company. We provide home inspection services to Winnipeg and surrounding areas. We bring a large knowledge base to the table.

Our Inspector are registered member of The Canadian Association of Home and Property Inspectors Manitoba, CAHPI-MB and strictly adheres to the Code of Ethics and Standards of Practice of CAHPI.

A home inspection is a visual non-invasive inspection of the homes major systems and components.
Our main goal as a home inspector is the well being of our clients. We are determined to eliminate buyers regret by one inspection at a time, by devoting 100% of our time and knowledge to each inspection. We will provide every client with a report that is an accurate reflection of any and all defects, big or small, that are occurring in the home.

Pre-sale and pre-purchase inspections are offered. Each inspection takes between 2-4 hrs to complete. Clients are welcome and encouraged to attend the inspection so that all the needs and concerns of the client can be addressed and discussed.
